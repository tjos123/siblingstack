Once both your kids are eating at the table, the high chair question shifts from "which one's best for my baby" to "do I need two chairs, or can one system handle both." There's genuinely a product built for exactly this — alongside several others that grow with a single child across enough stages that you may only need to buy once per kid.

*As always with gear roundups: confirm current specs and pricing on the retailer page before buying — models update and stock changes.*

## Quick picks

- **Best for seating two kids in one unit:** Graco Blossom 6-in-1
- **Best long-term single-child investment:** Graco Table2Table Premier Fold 7-in-1
- **Best for small spaces:** Stokke Clikk or Evrloop 3-in-1
- **Best tested-and-rated overall:** Munchkin 360° Cloud

## Graco Blossom 6-in-1

This is the one genuinely built for your specific situation: it explicitly supports seating **two children at once**, in three configurations — high chair plus toddler booster, infant booster plus youth stool, or toddler booster plus youth stool. For a family with two kids close enough in age to be eating together but at different stages, this is a single furniture purchase that adapts to both kids simultaneously rather than needing two chairs bought at two different times.

It also converts through six total stages, so it has a long runway with either kid individually.

## Graco Table2Table Premier Fold 7-in-1

If you'd rather have two separate units that each have a long individual lifespan, the Table2Table converts from infant high chair through toddler chair, booster, and eventually a standalone kids' table-and-chair set. The "eventually becomes furniture" stage matters for sibling households: the first kid's outgrown high chair doesn't need to be stored or sold, it just becomes their actual chair, freeing the original high-chair slot for the second kid.

## Stokke Clikk / Evrloop 3-in-1

Both built around a small footprint and quick assembly — the Clikk clicks together without tools, and the Evrloop (made from recycled plastic) converts from baby seat to toddler booster without tools either. Worth prioritising if kitchen or dining space is tight, which becomes a more pressing constraint once you're fitting two kids' seating at one table rather than one.

## Munchkin 360° Cloud

Consistently rated highly across independent testing panels for ease of use and cleaning — relevant when you're cleaning up after two kids' worth of mealtime mess rather than one. A dishwasher-safe tray insert saves real time across hundreds of meals.

## What matters most with two kids close in age

**Tray and seat-pad cleanability outweighs almost every other feature** — you'll be cleaning these multiple times a day. **Check the actual footprint of any "seats two kids" unit against your real dining space** before buying — combined units are sometimes wider than two standalone boosters, despite being marketed as the space-saving option.
